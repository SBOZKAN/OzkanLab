Dynamic Coupling Index (DCI)

The Dynamic Coupling Index (DCI) evaluates the allosteric coupling between residue pairs within a protein. Similar to DFI, DCI uses perturbation responses to gauge how changes in one part of a protein affect distant regions. High DCI scores indicate strong allosteric communication, essential for understanding how distant mutations can impact active sites and overall protein function.
